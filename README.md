# MSc_Craniofacial_NC_enhancers
This repository contains scripts and Jupiter notebooks used for my master thesis in Evolutionary Genomics with the title "Regulatory network evolution of neural crest-derived phenotypic traits across mammals"



This thesis are based on in-lab generated and publicaly available datasets integration. Studies from which the data was used:
1) Multiple seqience alignment - [Zoonomia Consoctium, 2020](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-020-2876-6)
2) GWAS of human facial variation - [Goovaerts et al., 2026 The craniofacial shape of modern humans embodies genomic signatures of evolution, diversity, and clinical conditions](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2025.12.29.696848v2)
3) Enhancer coordinates - in-lab analysis, preprint will be published soon
